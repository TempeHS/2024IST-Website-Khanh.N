Banh Mi Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • 200g sliced pork or chicken
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 4-6 fresh chili peppers, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Soy sauce
  • Maggi seasoning sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the baguette in half lengthwise and toast it in the oven until crispy.
  2. In a skillet, cook the sliced pork or chicken with soy sauce, Maggi seasoning sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on both sides of the toasted baguette.
  4. Layer the cooked pork or chicken, julienned carrots, cucumber slices, fresh cilantro, and chili peppers (if desired) on one side of the baguette.
  5. Close the sandwich with the other half of the baguette.
  6. Press the sandwich gently to compact the filling.
  7. Cut the sandwich into individual servings and serve immediately.
